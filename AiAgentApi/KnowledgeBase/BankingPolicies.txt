Chapter 1 - Credit Card Benefits

Premium customers receive unlimited lounge access and cashback up to 5%.

Regular customers receive cashback up to 1%.


Chapter 2 - Insurance Benefits

Premium customers receive complimentary life insurance coverage up to 25 lakhs.

Regular customers receive coverage up to 5 lakhs.


Chapter 3 - Premium Loan Policy

Premium customers can receive loans up to 50 lakhs subject to income validation, risk assessment, and repayment capability review.

Regular customers can receive loans up to 10 lakhs.


Chapter 4 - KYC Requirements

Customers must provide Aadhaar, PAN, and address proof for verification.

KYC verification is mandatory before account activation.
